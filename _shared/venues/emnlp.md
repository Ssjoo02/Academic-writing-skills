# EMNLP Venue Profile

## Venue Kind

- conference

## Source Status

- Official sources:
  - EMNLP 2026 Main Conference CFP: https://2026.emnlp.org/calls/main_conference_papers/
  - ACL Rolling Review CFP: https://aclrollingreview.org/cfp
  - ACLPUB Paper Formatting Guidelines: https://acl-org.github.io/ACLPUB/formatting.html
- Access date: 2026-06-12
- Verified for: EMNLP 2026 main conference plus ARR submission requirements.
- Drift risk: medium; recheck the official EMNLP and ARR pages before final submission.

## Scope

- Applies to: EMNLP 2026 main conference long and short papers.
- Submission version: ARR review submission; accepted final versions receive a separate
  camera-ready allowance.

## Length And Counting

- Submission content limit: long papers 8 pages; short papers 4 pages.
- Camera-ready allowance: accepted long papers up to 9 pages; accepted short papers up to 5 pages.
- References count: no under the shared ACL pattern.
- Appendix count: no under the shared ACL / ARR pattern, but appendices are optional reviewer
  material and should remain nonessential.
- Checklist count: responsible NLP research checklist required through ARR / ACL policy handling.
- Ethics/limitations count: limitations are required after the conclusion and do not count toward the
  content-page limit; optional ethical considerations also sit outside the main content budget under
  ARR wording.
- Supplementary material count: not verified.

## Post-Main Order

- Required order: main content, `Limitations`, optional ethical considerations, references,
  appendices / supplementary material as allowed by the submission system.
- Optional sections: responsible NLP checklist, ethics/disclosure material, supplementary material.
- Not verified: final submission-system packaging details for supplementary material and checklist
  metadata.

## Drafting Implications

- Select long or short paper before page budgeting.
- Resolve draft stage before page budgeting: submission uses 8 pages for long papers and 4 pages for
  short papers; camera-ready uses 9 / 5 only after acceptance or explicit camera-ready instruction.
- Do not use the camera-ready allowance as the submission-stage budget.
- Add Open Decisions for responsible NLP checklist details, disclosure policy, data/code
  availability, and supplementary material when the project context requires them.
- Keep the review paper self-contained; put nonessential detail in appendices or supplementary material.
- Audit all author-identifying content under ARR two-way anonymized review.

## Final Gate

- Before submission-ready: recheck the current official EMNLP and ARR pages for deadline-specific
  packaging details, checklist handling, and supplementary material policy.

## Do Not Infer

- Do not infer EMNLP-specific empirical-story preferences or current-year submission requirements from the 2025 sources.
