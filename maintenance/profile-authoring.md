# Profile Authoring

This file guides maintainers when converting external material into skill references.

`static/workflow/full-draft.md` and `static/workflow/draft-revision.md` are
runtime workflow and loading guidance. `references/principles/research-strategy.md` and
`references/sections/*.md` are runtime writing guidance. `maintenance/profile-authoring.md` is
maintenance guidance for expanding the skill.

Before using any extracted principle or profile, record its provenance in
`maintenance/source-provenance.md`.

## Allowed Extraction

From public writing advice:

- general writing principles,
- failure signals,
- revision actions,
- reviewer-facing reasoning,
- claim/evidence discipline.

From official venue material:

- formatting constraints,
- author guidance,
- submission expectations,
- page or anonymity requirements when relevant to writing strategy.

From representative papers:

- problem-framing moves,
- gap construction,
- insight placement,
- method reveal timing,
- evidence sequence,
- figure/table role,
- limitation handling.

## Disallowed Extraction

- Do not copy distinctive phrasing.
- Do not copy sentences or paragraphs.
- Do not turn one paper's prose into a reusable template.
- Do not claim a citation supports a point unless verified.
- Do not invent venue preferences when sources are missing.

## Profile Card Format

Use this shape for patterns extracted from representative papers:

```markdown
## Pattern: [Pattern Name]
- Use when:
- Opening move:
- Gap construction:
- Insight placement:
- Method reveal timing:
- Evidence sequence:
- Figure/table role:
- Claims to avoid:
- Reviewer risk reduced:
```
