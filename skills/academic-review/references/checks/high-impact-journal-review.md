# High-Impact Journal Review Check

Use this reference for Nature-family, Science-family, Cell-family, or similar
broad-audience journal targets. It adds broad-interest and editorial-risk axes to
the normal reviewer-risk check; it does not replace technical review.

## Review Axes

Assess the manuscript against:

| Axis | Reviewer question | Typical fix |
|---|---|---|
| originality | What is new beyond known methods, datasets, or applications? | sharpen the insight and non-claims |
| scientific importance | Why should the result matter beyond the immediate subfield? | connect to field-level consequence supported by evidence |
| interdisciplinary readership | Can scientists outside the specialty follow the problem and stakes? | define terms, move narrow setup later, improve summary paragraph |
| technical soundness | Are methods, controls, statistics, and availability strong enough? | add or request real analysis; otherwise weaken claims |
| readability for nonspecialists | Does the abstract/introduction explain the finding before jargon? | rewrite the reader funnel and captions |

## Procedure

1. Run the normal two-round review first.
2. Re-score the central claim under the five axes above.
3. Identify which axis would cause editorial triage or reviewer rejection.
4. Map each issue to `PROSE_REVISION`, `CLAIM_WEAKENING`, `CITATION_VERIFICATION`,
   `ADDITIONAL_ANALYSIS`, `NEW_EXPERIMENT`, `OPEN_DECISION`, or `BLOCKED`.
5. For Nature-family targets, check the active venue card for summary paragraph,
   display tier, Methods placement, Reporting Summary, and Data/Code Availability.

## Limits

- Do not claim editor fit as settled.
- Do not call a manuscript Nature-ready merely because it is well written.
- Do not inflate significance beyond supplied evidence.
- Do not hide a missing experiment as a writing problem.

## Output

Report:

- strongest broad-interest case,
- weakest review axis,
- blockers before a serious high-impact journal submission,
- concrete manuscript deltas,
- missing evidence or venue facts.
