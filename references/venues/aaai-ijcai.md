# AAAI/IJCAI Venue Profile

## Venue Kind

- conference

## Source Status

- Official sources:
  - AAAI-26 Main Technical Track CFP: https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/main-technical-track-call/
  - AAAI-26 Submission Instructions: https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/submission-instructions/
  - AAAI-26 Author Kit: https://aaai.org/conference/aaai/aaai-26/author-kit/
  - IJCAI-ECAI 2026 Main Track CFP: https://2026.ijcai.org/ijcai-ecai-2026-call-for-papers-main-track/
- Access date: 2026-06-08
- Verified for: AAAI-26 and IJCAI-ECAI 2026 main-track planning.
- Drift risk: high; AAAI and IJCAI rules differ and must not be merged.

## Scope

- Applies to: AAAI or IJCAI only after the selected target is explicit.
- Submission version: review submission; camera-ready deltas must be rechecked.

## Length And Counting

- AAAI main text limit: up to 7 pages of technical content.
- AAAI references count: no, when extra pages contain references only.
- AAAI appendix count: separate supplementary technical appendix; reviewers are not required to review it.
- AAAI checklist count: no; the reproducibility checklist is placed after references in the submitted PDF.
- AAAI ethics/limitations count: not verified as a separate page-count exemption.
- IJCAI main text limit: 7 body pages including figures and tables.
- IJCAI references count: up to 2 additional reference pages within the 9-page total.
- IJCAI appendix count: not verified for the main PDF; use supplementary material only after checking the current CFP.
- IJCAI checklist count: not verified.
- IJCAI ethics/limitations count: optional ethics statement may appear in the body or reference pages.

## Post-Main Order

- AAAI required order: main technical content, references, reproducibility checklist.
- IJCAI required order: main body, references; ethics placement may be in body or reference pages.
- Optional sections: AAAI supplementary technical appendix, multimedia, and code/data; IJCAI supplementary material.
- Not verified: exact limitations placement for either venue.

## Drafting Implications

- Ask whether the target is AAAI or IJCAI before final page budgeting.
- For AAAI, use 7 technical-content pages plus references and reserve a checklist slot after references.
- For IJCAI, use 7 body pages plus up to 2 reference pages and keep optional ethics placement explicit.
- Keep supplementary material nonessential for both venues and audit all submitted materials for anonymity.

## Final Gate

- Before submission-ready: confirm the selected venue, official template, page counting, post-main order, checklist or ethics requirements, supplementary rules, and anonymity against the current official page.

## Do Not Infer

- Do not merge AAAI and IJCAI rules; when a field differs, use only the selected venue's official facts.
