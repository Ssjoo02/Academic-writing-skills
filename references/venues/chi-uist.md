## Source Status

- Official sources:
  - CHI 2026 Papers CFP: https://chi2026.acm.org/authors/papers/
  - CHI 2026 Publication Formats: https://chi2026.acm.org/chi-publication-formats/
  - UIST 2026 Author Guide: https://uist.acm.org/2026/author-guide/
- Access date: 2026-06-08
- Verified for: CHI 2026 planning; only limited UIST 2026 author-guide checks.
- Drift risk: high; CHI and UIST must be handled separately.

## Scope

- Applies to: CHI papers. UIST uses this card only for approximate planning until current page and format facts are verified.
- Submission version: review submission; publication and camera-ready deltas must be rechecked.

## Length And Counting

- CHI main text limit: word-budget planning; short papers are 5,000 words or less, standard papers are 5,000 to 12,000 words.
- CHI references count: no.
- CHI appendix count: no for word-count planning, but appendices are optional for reviewers.
- CHI checklist count: accessibility checks are required by policy, but exact checklist handling in the paper package is not verified here.
- CHI ethics/limitations count: not verified as separate placement or count fields.
- CHI supplementary material count: not verified; treat as optional support.
- UIST fields: page or word limit not verified.

## Post-Main Order

- CHI required order: not verified beyond ACM review format expectations.
- Optional sections: appendices and supplementary material.
- Not verified: CHI post-main statement order, UIST page/word limits, UIST post-main order, and UIST checklist or appendix rules.

## Drafting Implications

- Ask whether the target is CHI or UIST before final template and length checks.
- For CHI, use word-budget planning rather than page-budget planning and keep all central claims in the main paper.
- Add CHI Open Decisions for short versus standard length, accessibility checks, anonymized links, optional video figures, and supplementary material.
- For UIST, add novelty/new-content and production-quality checks based on the author guide.

## Final Gate

- Before submission-ready: confirm the selected venue's current CFP, official template, length counting, post-main order, accessibility or checklist handling, supplement rules, anonymized links, and publication workflow requirements.

## Do Not Infer

- Do not infer UIST page, word, appendix, or checklist rules from CHI.
