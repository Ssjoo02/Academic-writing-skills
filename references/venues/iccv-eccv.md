- ECCV main text limit: 14 pages including figures and tables.
- ECCV references count: no, when extra pages contain only cited references.
- ECCV appendix count: appendices are not permitted in the main submission.
- ECCV checklist count: not verified.
- ECCV ethics/limitations count: not verified as separate page-count exemptions.
- ECCV supplementary material count: optional supplementary material is separate from the main submission.
- ICCV fields: not verified.

## Post-Main Order

- ECCV required order: main paper, cited references only.
- ECCV optional sections: supplementary material outside the main submission.
- Not verified: current ICCV page budget, template, appendix, supplementary, checklist, ethics, and limitations rules.

## Drafting Implications

- For ECCV planning, use a 14-page Page Budget Summary in LNCS style and keep cited references outside that budget.
- Do not put appendices in the ECCV main submission; move optional support to supplementary material and keep the paper self-contained.
- Add an Open Decision for the selected ECCV contribution type.
- For ICCV planning, verify the current ICCV author guideline before setting page budget, template, or supplementary rules.

## Final Gate

For ECCV, confirm the current author kit, page limit, references-only pages, no-main-appendix rule, supplementary rules, external-link rules, and double-blind requirements.
