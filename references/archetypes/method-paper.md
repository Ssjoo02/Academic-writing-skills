# Method Paper Archetype

## Story Logic

- Start from an important problem or capability gap.
- Explain why prior approaches fail or remain insufficient.
- Present the core insight before listing implementation details.
- Introduce the method as a consequence of the insight.
- Use experiments to prove effectiveness, causality, and limits.

## Framework Effects

- Abstract: problem, gap, method idea, main evidence, scoped conclusion.
- Introduction: problem-first narrative, prior-work tension, key insight, contribution, evidence preview.
- Method: motivate every module by a claim or failure mode.
- Experiments: main comparison, ablation, robustness/generalization, failure analysis.
- Related Work: contrast method families and locate the gap.
- Conclusion: restate contribution and limits without inflating scope.

## Reviewer Risks

- Looks like an incremental module addition.
- Main gain is small or predictable.
- Ablation does not isolate the claimed insight.
- Baselines are weak or unfair.
